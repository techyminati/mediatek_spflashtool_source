¡¤           BOOTLOADER Region             

–     PRELOADER, DSP_BL

–     Request: Update ALL or Skip All to update

–     Exception: Partial Update

 

¡¤           Protected Invisible Region

–     __NODL_NVRAM, __NODL_PRO_INFO, __NODL_PROTECT_F, __NODL_PROTECT_S

–     Request: Protect data along with firmware upgrade process

–     Exception: data lost, __NODL_NVRAM is not found.

NOTE:

__NODL_NVRAM is required to exist, if not, error message will pop up and firmware upgrade will be stopped.

–     Exception: Wrongly erase

 

¡¤           Normal Visible Region

–     UBOOT, BOOTIMG, ANDROID and so on.

–     Request: Erase old data on target by PMT, Download new data to target

–     Exception: Wrongly erase or download

¡¤           Kept Visible Region

–     FAT, (Maybe USRDATA¡­)

–     Request: Location not change among firmware upgrade

–     Location not change among firmware upgrade, could be skipped to update

–     Exception: Wrongly erase or download

 

¡¤           Reserved Region

–     __RSV_NODL_OTP, __NODL_PMT, __NODL_BMTPOOL
